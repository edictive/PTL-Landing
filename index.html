The underlying insight driving Predictive Text Labs is that when leaders ask “what should I do?”, they want an actionable answer, not another chart. Anyone who's worked with a CEO knows that actionable answers start with a forecast—and forecasts require understanding patterns in time.

LLMs are powerful; trained on billions of articles, books, and websites, they summarize the past brilliantly. But we still don’t trust them to forecast.

That's as future decisions don’t run on text. They run on time series: sales data, sensor readings, and market movements. And understanding language is a fundamentally different capability from understanding time. Real forecasting requires seeing how events cascade, why patterns repeat, and which signals matter; seeing time as a continuous flow, not disconnected points.

We believe temporal reasoning isn’t just another AI capability—it’s the core primitive that turns LLMs from search engines into decision-making partners.

Without it, AI can analyze the past but never predict the future.

This temporal gap limits every organization trying to leverage AI to drive decisions.

Our mission is to close it.
